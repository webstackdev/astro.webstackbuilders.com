# Cookie Consent Component

The Cookies component provides a GDPR-compliant cookie consent banner with customizable options and preferences.

import Cookies from '@components/Cookies/index.astro'

## Default Cookie Banner

<Cookies />

## Cookie Banner with Preferences

<Cookies
  showPreferences={true}
  allowPartialAccept={true}
/>

## Minimal Cookie Notice

<Cookies
  variant="minimal"
  message="We use cookies to enhance your experience."
/>

## Usage

The Cookies component provides:

- GDPR compliance
- Granular cookie preferences
- Customizable messaging
- Persistent consent storage
- Analytics integration

```astro
---
import Cookies from '@components/Cookies/index.astro'
---

<!-- Basic cookie consent -->
<Cookies />

<!-- With preference controls -->
<Cookies
  showPreferences={true}
  allowPartialAccept={true}
/>
```

## Cookie Categories

The component supports different cookie categories:

- **Necessary**: Essential cookies for site functionality
- **Analytics**: Usage tracking and performance monitoring
- **Marketing**: Advertising and personalization cookies
- **Functional**: Enhanced features and preferences

## Features

- **GDPR Compliant**: Meets European privacy regulations
- **Granular Control**: Users can accept/reject specific cookie types
- **Customizable**: Configurable messages and styling
- **Persistent**: Remembers user preferences across sessions
- **Accessible**: Screen reader friendly with proper ARIA labels

## Props

- **showPreferences** (optional): Display detailed cookie preferences
- **allowPartialAccept** (optional): Allow users to accept only some cookies
- **variant**: "default" or "minimal" styling
- **message** (optional): Custom consent message
- **position**: Banner position - "bottom" (default) or "top"
