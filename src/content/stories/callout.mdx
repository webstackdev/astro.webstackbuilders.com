# Callout Component

Callout components are used to highlight important information, warnings, tips, and other content that needs special attention.

import Callout from '@components/Callout/index.astro'

## Info Callout

<Callout type="info" title="Information">
  This is an informational callout to provide helpful context to users.
</Callout>

## Success Callout

<Callout type="success" title="Success">
  Great! Your action was completed successfully.
</Callout>

## Warning Callout

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
  Please be careful when performing this action as it cannot be undone.
</Callout>

## Error Callout

<Callout type="error" title="Error">
  Something went wrong. Please check your input and try again.
</Callout>

## Callout without Title

<Callout type="info">
  This callout doesn't have a title, just the content message.
</Callout>

## Usage

```astro
---
import Callout from '@components/Callout/index.astro'
---

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
  Your callout content goes here.
</Callout>
```
